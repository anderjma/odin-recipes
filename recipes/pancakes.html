Pancakes

Description:

Fluffy homemade pancakes that are perfect for breakfast and easy to make with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or toppings of your choice.
